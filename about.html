Welcome to RNG Hospital

Best Medical & Health care Needs to Our Patients

In the short span of its existence, RNG Hospital has created its niche on the map of quality healthcare delivery in West Bengal by touching nearly 2 million lives. Having earned the trust and loyalty of patients through the compassion of its caregivers and high-precision medical technology, the institution has emerged as the leading quaternary care facility in the region.

The hospital is well laid out in a 40 acre green landscape in the serene setting of RANIGANJ and is the closest facility to the Kazi Nazrul Islam Airport,DURGAPUR.
